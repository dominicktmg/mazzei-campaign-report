## Report Structure
1. **Hero Stats** — 4 key metrics (impressions, clicks, CPC, engagement)
2. **Campaign Wins** — 6 cards highlighting successes with metric badges
3. **Performance vs. Benchmarks** — 6 comparison cards
4. **Top Performing Content** — Table of best content themes
5. **Reach & Traffic Sources** — Geographic breakdown + channel mix
6. **Next Phase Strategy** — Forward-looking opportunities (NOT "areas for consideration" — keep it proactive, not critical)
7. **Bottom Line** — 3-point executive summary

## Important Framing Notes
- Always position as wins and forward momentum
- "Next Phase Strategy" should be proactive ideas, NOT problems or weaknesses
- Client should never feel like we're pointing out issues — show we're already thinking ahead
- Frame everything as "here's our roadmap" not "here's what's broken"
